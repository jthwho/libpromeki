Detailed Description

Manages a list of strings.

Derives from List<String> and adds string-specific helpers such as join() and filter(). Overrides the base _promeki_clone() and the Ptr alias so SharedPtr<StringList> behaves correctly under copy-on-write — without these, copy-on-write would slice the StringList back to a plain List<String> at the next modify() call.

Thread Safety
Inherits List — distinct instances may be used concurrently; concurrent access to a single instance must be externally synchronized.
