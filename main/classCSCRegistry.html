Detailed Description

Static registry for fast-path CSC conversion kernels.

Maps (source PixelDesc, target PixelDesc) pairs to hand-tuned conversion functions that bypass the general pipeline. Register fast paths at static-init time; they are discovered automatically by CSCPipeline during compilation.
