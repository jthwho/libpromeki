Detailed Description

One directed frame-flow edge between two stages.

At start() time the pipeline connects the from stage's frameReadySignal to a handler that drains its ready queue into the to stage's writeFrame. Fan-out is implicit — a source may appear as from in multiple routes; each destination back-pressures independently.

fromTrack and toTrack are reserved for future multi-track stages and ignored today.
