Detailed Description

HostBufferImpl variant with page locking and secure zeroing.

Backs MemSpace::SystemSecure. On construction the allocation is page-locked via secureLock so its contents do not get swapped to disk. On destruction the buffer is secureZero'd before being unlocked and freed, matching the old MemSpace::Ops behavior.
