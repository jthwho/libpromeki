Detailed Description

A single entry in the CIE 1931 wavelength lookup table.

Each entry maps a wavelength of electromagnetic radiation (in nanometers) to its corresponding CIE 1931 XYZ tristimulus values and xy chromaticity coordinates. These values come from the CIE 1931 2-degree standard observer color matching functions, which model how the three types of cone cells in the human retina respond to light of different wavelengths.
