Detailed Description

Audio signal generator for producing test tones and silence.

Generates multi-channel audio data based on per-channel configuration. Each channel can independently produce silence or a sine tone at a configurable frequency and level.

Example
AudioDesc desc(48000, 2);
AudioGen gen(desc);
gen.setConfig(0, { AudioGen::Sine, 1000.0f, AudioLevel::fromDbfs(-10.0), 0.0f, 0.0f });
Audio audio = gen.generate(4800);
