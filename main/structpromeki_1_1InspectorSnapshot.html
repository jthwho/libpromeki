Detailed Description

Aggregate snapshot of the inspector's accumulated state.

InspectorMediaIO::snapshot returns one of these on demand. Counters are monotonic over the inspector's lifetime; the lastEvent field is the most recently produced InspectorEvent (a copy, taken under the inspector's mutex).
