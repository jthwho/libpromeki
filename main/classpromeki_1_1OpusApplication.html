Detailed Description

Well-known Enum type for the Opus encoder application mode.

Maps directly onto libopus's OPUS_APPLICATION_* constants:

  • Voip → OPUS_APPLICATION_VOIP — best quality at a given bitrate for voice; emphasises voice intelligibility over musical fidelity.
  • Audio → OPUS_APPLICATION_AUDIO — best quality at a given bitrate for music and most non-voice content.
  • LowDelay → OPUS_APPLICATION_RESTRICTED_LOWDELAY — disables the speech-optimised mode and drops the additional look-ahead, lowering algorithmic delay at a small quality cost.

Selected via MediaConfig::OpusApplication; the codec default is Audio so general-purpose pipelines get full-fidelity behaviour without explicit configuration.
