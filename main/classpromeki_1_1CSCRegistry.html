Detailed Description

Static registry for fast-path CSC conversion kernels.

Maps (source PixelFormat, target PixelFormat) pairs to hand-tuned conversion functions that bypass the general pipeline. Register fast paths at static-init time; they are discovered automatically by CSCPipeline during compilation.

Thread Safety
Fully thread-safe. Registrations are expected at static-init time and the registry is internally synchronized; thereafter lookup is lock-free.
