Constructs a FrameSync-based SDL player MediaIO.

Creates an SDLPlayerTask with the supplied widget/audio pointers and adopts it into a newly allocated MediaIO. The returned MediaIO owns the task; video and audio remain caller-owned and must outlive the returned MediaIO.

Parameters
videoVideo widget (may be nullptr).
audioAudio output (may be nullptr).
useAudioClockPrefer the audio device as the timing source.
parentOptional parent object.
Returns
A new MediaIO ready to be configured and opened as a writer.
