Detailed Description

Result of decoding one band.

error is the canonical "did this band decode" check. The other fields are populated when error.isOk() and left at default values otherwise; bitWidth and syncStartCol are also populated for diagnostic purposes when the sync nibble was located but a later step failed (e.g. CRC mismatch).
