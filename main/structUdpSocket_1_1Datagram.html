Detailed Description

Describes a single datagram for batch-send APIs.

Used by writeDatagrams() to submit many datagrams in one syscall. The data pointer must remain valid until the call returns. When txTimeNs is non-zero and the socket has transmit-time enabled, the kernel (via the ETF qdisc) will hold the packet until the requested send time.
