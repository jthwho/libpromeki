Detailed Description

Descriptor for a single media track in the container.

Tracks are populated by the engine during open() (for readers) or addTrack()/writeSample() (for writers). Callers observe track properties through the const accessors; the non-const setters exist for the engine's own use and are not part of the normal consumer API.
